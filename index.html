Reflection

Design Thinking Process on Food Waste: Applying the Design Thinking process to food waste involves understanding stakeholder perspectives, reframing the problem broadly, generating creative solutions, prototyping ideas, and gathering feedback iteratively. Challenges include the complexity of the issue, scaling solutions, and fostering behavior change. Success requires ongoing collaboration and adaptation.

Market and Careers of Software Development in KRG:The software development market in the Kurdistan Region of Iraq (KRG) shows promising growth potential, driven by increasing demand for digital solutions locally and globally. However, challenges such as limited infrastructure and access to resources persist. Careers in software development offer opportunities for skilled professionals, but there is a need for continuous skill development and adaptation to evolving technologies to stay competitive in the market.

Visit To Korek Telecom Company : Transitioning from 4G to 5G marks a revolutionary shift in telecommunications, promising speeds up to 10 Gbps and reduced latency to 1 millisecond. Our visit to Korek Telecom's server room revealed meticulous organization and backup systems, showcasing their commitment to reliability in this transformative era
